Welcome To My Homepage

by Alexa De La Garza

Hello! My name is Alexa De La Garza. I am a Latina and a First-generation undergraduate student completing her B.S. in Computer Engineering Technology from the University of Houston. My interests include Data Analytics, Geo-Data, Machine Learning, Image Processing, and Automation!

At the moment of writing this, I find myself a couple months away from graduating. I created this website because I would like to have a place where I can share my work and experiences as I balance my education, work, and my extracurricular activities. It is a dream of mine to be able to inspire students of different backgrounds and experiences to pursue a career in any STEM related field.

If you have any questions or would like to simply strike up a conversation, please reach out!
